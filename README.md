S.P.A.R.K. Academy (Supporting Progress and Rising Knowledge) is a free online initiative I created to provide access to high-quality educational classes for teenagers and schoolchildren from around the world. The academy operates on a volunteer basis: a team of visiting teachers conducts online lessons in English and other subjects, and students can join for free and with no entry requirements.

As the founder and director of the project, I am responsible for organizing the academy: finding volunteer teachers, coordinating the schedule, communicating with students, forming groups, and monitoring the quality of instruction. My goal is to create a convenient and supportive educational environment where students can develop regardless of their financial situation or location.

S.P.A.R.K. Academy focuses on short, effective courses and conversation clubs that help students improve their knowledge and confidence. The project also creates a community of motivated teenagers willing to support each other in their learning and personal growth.

I also invite you to participate in this project together, becoming a teacher or a student. This project is also very suitable for those applying to colleges and universities in the US. It's a great volunteer program, and upon completion, you'll receive a certificate of completion from this school, which will be a plus for your portfolio.
